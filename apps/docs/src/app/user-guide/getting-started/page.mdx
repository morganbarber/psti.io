# Getting Started

This guide will help you get started with psti.io, from creating an account to making your first paste.

## Creating an Account

1. Navigate to the psti.io homepage
2. Click the **Sign Up** button in the top right corner
3. Enter your email address and create a strong password
4. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox
5. Log in with your credentials

> **Note:** You can create pastes without an account, but creating an account allows you to manage, edit, and organize your pastes.

## Logging In

1. Click the **Log In** button on the homepage
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Sign In**

### Password Reset

If you forget your password:

1. Click **Forgot Password** on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a password reset link
4. Follow the link and create a new password

## Understanding the Interface

### Navigation Bar

- **Home** - Return to the main page
- **My Pastes** - View all your pastes (requires login)
- **New Paste** - Create a new paste
- **Profile** - Manage your account settings

### Dashboard

After logging in, your dashboard shows:

- Recent pastes
- Paste statistics
- Quick actions (create, search, organize)

## Creating Your First Paste

1. Click **New Paste** in the navigation bar
2. Enter your content in the editor
3. Choose a syntax highlighting language (optional)
4. Set privacy level (see [Privacy Settings](/user-guide/privacy-settings))
5. Click **Create Paste**

That's it! You've created your first paste. You'll be redirected to your paste's unique URL.
